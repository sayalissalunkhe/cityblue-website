About Us

Overview

CityBlue Technologies is considered one of the most well-rounded solutions for the architectural, engineering, and manufacturing industries. By providing top-of-the-line CAD Products and Supplies, CityBlue Technologies is tough to beat. Our CAD Products consist of our own private label product line along with Oce paper and toner products, RSA (Reprographic Services Association) paper products, as well as all of your Canon ink needs. These high quality products help deliver the total solution.

CityBlue Technologies partners with the industry leading companies below to provide you with latest large format printing technology. We pair the latest technology with our expert sales staff and our experienced service team to provide the best possible service to our customers. Together with our strategic partners CityBlue Technologies truly delivers solutions beyond expectations.

Lifecycle Solutions

Commodities

The foundation of the drawing process is paper, toner and ink products. CityBlue offers every major line of OEM products along with CityBlue branded paper products. Clients have the option of ordering either live with an account executive or via a fully functional web presence. Supplemental products such as manual drafting supplies are also available providing the client an opportunity to have a single point of purchase for their entire commodity needs.

Equipment

CityBlue offers the industry leading Oce product line as our primary equipment offering. CityBlue also offers Canon,and KIP as supporting products should another line better fit a client’s needs. We lease and sell equipment from printers to copiers and scanners among others. CityBlue has an industry leading refurbished program allowing customers to gain access to a more capable piece of equipment at a lower capital entry point. In addition, CityBlue offers a ‘Pay as You Print’ program for those clients who do not want to purchase equipment.

Service and Support

CityBlue offers telephone support and service contracts for maintenance and material for equipment supplied by it.

Digital Printing

CityBlue provides architectural, engineering and construction firms with high quality digital blueprints used for design, bidding and construction of commercial and residential construction. As part of providing this service, CityBlue also offers Bid Document Management services that facilitate the management of the bid process for the owner or lead architect of a construction project.

Digital Services

CityBlue can electronically store, manage and allow web viewing, printing and retrieval of any drawing or specification for any project. In addition, the bid process can be managed electronically, from pre qualification through monitoring, allowing even greater visibility to any project anywhere and anytime.

Color Printing

CityBlue provides color options for architectural renderings. In addition, it also provides high end color solutions for its clients marketing requirements by providing posters, banners, trade show graphics, theater promotions and other color printing applications to education institutions, medical facilities and advertising agencies.

History

Business professionals have benefited from CityBlue's diverse line of high-tech products, services and solutions for over four decades.

Founded in 1968 as a traditional blueprint company, CityBlue Print and Supply, quickly grew into a mid-sized family business with offices in Peoria, IL and Moline, IL.

The guiding principle of exceeding expectations, holds true even today. CityBlue Technologies (then City Blueprint & Supply Company) began by providing reprographic services, engineering supplies and drafting services. Since then, CityBlue has evolved into a very diverse company that offers a variety of high-tech products, services and solutions to professionals in Architecture, Engineering, Manufacturing, Automotive, Aerospace, Utilities, Government and more.

Corporate Social Responsibility

CityBlue’s corporate philosophy is deeply rooted in principles of sustainability, recognizing and adopting practices that encompass environmental stewardship, adding economic value and providing a safe and healthy work environment for its employees.

CityBlue uses low CFC output devices and substrates that are bio-degradable. In addition, it has an energy star certified product line.

City Blue has a range of products for client use that are environmentally friendly, such as plotter paper and xerographic media. Our printing recycle program has paperless options and the choice of printing on recycled material. CityBlue’s ‘Pay as You Print’ program is also targeted at minimizing wastage by printing only the exact quantities required. In keeping with its commitment to sustainable development, five percent of all green sales are donated to the Arbor Day Foundation.
