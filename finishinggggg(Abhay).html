All the Finishing Touches

Finishing Services

First impressions count. Need some finishing touches for your presentations, artwork, posters, or pictures? CityBlue
Technologies provides convenient mounting and laminating (up to 36” wide) to give your pieces a durable, completed
look. We also offer comb binding, booklet making, stapling and strip binding for your prints.



We offer various options in mounting substrates (foamcore, gator board, Coroplast, Styrene, Tyvek, Sintra and
Masonite among others) and laminating finishes (matte, glossy, satin, and more).
